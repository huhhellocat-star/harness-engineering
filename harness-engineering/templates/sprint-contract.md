## Quality Thresholds

| Category | Minimum Score | Weight |
|----------|--------------|--------|
| Feature completeness | 8/10 | High |
| Functionality (no blocking bugs) | 9/10 | High |
| Design quality | 7/10 | Medium |
| Code quality | 7/10 | Medium |

If any category falls below its minimum, the sprint fails and returns to the builder.
